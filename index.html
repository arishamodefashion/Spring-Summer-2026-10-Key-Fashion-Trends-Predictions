1

Dots·Dots·Dots

Small dots, big dots, dotted everything. Playful yet sophisticated, retro yet minimal. Big Dots expected visibility: +55% EU and +33% US (SS26 vs prior year). Among women in EU, Big Dot Dresses skyrocket +80%, dotted skirts +47% — especially midi lengths.

1. CARO EDITION / 2-6. INSTAGRAM | Runway: Altuzarra, Khaite, Nina Ricci, Junya Watanabe, MM6, Jean Paul Gaultier

Key forecast: Dotted midi dresses and skirts lead the lady-like silhouette. Designers from Altuzarra to Jean Paul Gaultier embraced polka variations. The print balances whimsy and minimalism, dominating both Europe and America.

AltuzarraKhaiteNina RicciJunya WatanabeMM6Jean Paul Gaultier
SS26 Fashion Week impact: major dots revival
2

Raw Authenticity in Brut Denim

Unwashed, untreated, pure stiff cotton. No distressing, no fades — authentic raw denim. Head-to-toe matching denim looks (baggy jeans + jacket) surge globally. Fitted denim jacket (+5% EU) paired with flared bootcut jeans (+2% EU) gains traction.

1. EGONLAB / 2. GIVENCHY — raw denim on baggy jeans + matching jacket

Worldwide forecasts: Brut denim visibility +6% women / +3% men. +200% growth on runway (SS26 vs SS25). Casablanca, Givenchy, Dior, Zimmerman all featured raw denim collections. The "rising star" among edgy consumers.

3

Romantic Fabrics

Lace maxi skirts, satin trims, flowy lace pants, lace stockings — feminine silhouettes dominate. Lace skirts forecast: +20% EU, +13% US. Fine lace (+10%) and see-through textures (+5%) emerge for SS26.

Lace skirt + oversized pullover in vanilla yellow — delicate meets casual

Style tip: Pair midi lace skirts with oversized tees or modern pastel sweaters. The aesthetic balances subtle sensuality and relaxed elegance, crucial for SS26 wardrobes.

Trending: sheer layering, feminine silhouettes
4

Variations of Checked

Checked prints break seasonal boundaries — gingham (+17% men EU, +31% women EU, +22% women US). Prince of Wales checks (+19% EU women), Windowpane checks (+13% EU women) thrive on midi skirts, thin fitted jackets, and even swimwear.

Gingham dresses, short-sleeve shirts, boxers — seasonal dressing is fading

From picnic-ready gingham to Prince of Wales tailoring, checked variations bring versatility. Layering, midi lengths, and thinner blazers amplify the aesthetic in city looks.

📈 Gingham US women +22%👔 Prince of Wales +19%
5

Slipping into Moccasins

Loafers, boat shoes, moccasins — steady edgy staple. Miu Miu’s boat loafers, Chloé & Isabel Marant’s bohemian influence. Moccasins grew +40% among women (summer 2025) and remain stable in 2026. Men’s uptake +7% EU.

1. GIMAGUAS / 2. MORJAS / 3-4. MASSIMO DUTTI — bohemian-driven growth

This footwear trend bridges urban ease and heritage detailing. Suede finishes and fringe details lead, perfect for SS26 trans-seasonal styling.

🇪🇺 Men +7% | Women stable high visibility
6

Alternative Animal Prints

Zebra stripes leap +21% EU / +17% US (age 26-35 leading). Zebra print dresses +25% growth across regions. Snake & crocodile prints +22% EU. Cow print emerges as IT-print in US: +87% during SS26.

Brandon Maxwell SS26 | Diane Von Furstenberg SS26 runway

Leopard steps aside; zebra and Dalmatian-inspired dots take center stage. Cow print gains massive momentum in the US. Dresses remain the canvas of choice for bold wildlife prints.

Brandon MaxwellDVF
7

Creamy Yellow

Vanilla Yellow & Popcorn Yellow — new neutrals. Forecast: +22% women EU (both shades), Vanilla Yellow Dresses +23% EU women. Men’s steady growth: Popcorn Yellow +10%, Vanilla +7% across Europe.

1. H&M / 2. ARAKII / 3-4. HEURITECH — bridal to activewear

These buttery tones feel fresh on college-style sweaters, short-sleeve shirts, and even bridesmaids dresses. Versatile, soft, and universally flattering.

✨ Dresses +23% (EU)🍿 Popcorn Yellow men +10%
8

Ruffles & Bubble-Hem

Tiered ruffle skirts +20% EU / +17% US. Draped skirts, bounce bubble hems appear on skirts and sarouel pants. Optical white skirts (+14%) and low-waist styles (+17%) gain momentum. Playful volume meets airy spring aesthetic.

Tiered ruffles + low-waist — movement and lightness

The bubble hem trend expands beyond skirts to pants, creating sculptural silhouettes. White and pastel shades emphasize ethereal spring layering.

🎀 Bounce bubble hems + dramatic volume
9

Purple Hues

From burgundy-plum to luminous lilac — spiritual, elegant, expressive. Womenswear: Plum & Muted Berry +10% EU; Mauve (+2%) and Sangria Purple (+5%) in US. Menswear: Eggplant +7% EU, Vivid Purple +5%.

Ramadan celebrations, Paris runways, NY streets — individuality through violet

Purple signals emotional expression and individuality. Designers incorporate it from suiting to dreamy dresses. A colour that bridges streetwear and evening elegance.

Spiritual depthvibrant accents
10

The New Raw

Practical minimalism meets city mobility — sustainable, raw-functionality. Green hues: Pea Green (+13%), Pickled Green (+6%), Dusty Green (+4%). Opaque Rainproof +12% EU (women) & +6% US. Thong Sandals explode: +49% women EU, +11% US.

1. Pantone 2. Instagram 3. Pantone 4. Instagram — monochrome windbreakers + palazzo pants / cycling shorts
1. YIGAL AZROUËL / 2. KENT & CURWEN / 3. AUGUST BARRON / 4. BOTTEGA VENETA

Cycling-inspired urban wardrobe: funnel-neck windbreakers, mini cycling shorts (+16%), tube socks, yellow-green sneakers (+20%). Long-lasting, multi-purpose pieces with nature-infused palettes.

🚴‍♀️ Palazzo pants +37% | Balloon draped hems
🌿 Thong sandals EU women +49%

Urban living, elevated. The New Raw unites smart outdoor layers, timeless basics, and green-kissed yellow tones. Invest in quality, transitional pieces that reflect eco-conscious, cyclist-friendly city style.
